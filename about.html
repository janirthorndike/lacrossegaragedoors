About us

Local, Honest, Repair-First

La Crosse Garage Doors is a new, locally owned shop opening in August 2026 — built on the idea that most garage door problems are a fix, not a sale.

Why we started

The owner of La Crosse Garage Doors spent years running a garage door company in the mountains of Colorado — thousands of springs, openers, cables, and doors that wouldn't open on the coldest mornings. Moving to La Crosse meant a chance to bring that same hands-on, no-nonsense approach to the Coulee Region.

The idea is simple: lead with repair. A broken spring or a bad sensor is usually a same-day fix at a fair price — not a reason to sell you a whole new door. When a new door genuinely is the right call, we'll say so and help you choose well. When it isn't, we'll fix what you've got and get on with our day.

What "repair-first" means for you

  • An honest quote before any work starts — no surprise fees, no after-hours surcharge.
  • A real local person — not a call center, not a national lead-routing service with an 800 number.
  • Straight answers on repair vs. replace — including when the cheaper option is the right one.
  • Evenings and weekends at standard rates, because Wisconsin winters don't break springs on a 9-to-5 schedule.
